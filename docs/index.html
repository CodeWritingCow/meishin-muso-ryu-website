Meishin Muso Ryu

Meishin Muso Ryu (明神無想流) is an Iaido style passed down from Shin Kage Ryu (新陰流) which is currently at its 27th head instructor. Our head is Matsushita Shuji sensei. We are based in Stroudsburg, Pennsylvania.
